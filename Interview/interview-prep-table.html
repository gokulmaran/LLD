Managerial / Behavioural Questions 12 categories
CategorySample QuestionSTAR FrameworkWhat They're TestingTips
Leadership"Tell me about a time you led a team through a difficult project."Situation: project at risk → Task: lead recovery → Action: replanned, unblocked team → Result: shipped on timeOwnership, decisiveness, influenceShow impact in numbers
Conflict Resolution"Describe a conflict with a teammate and how you resolved it."Situation: disagreement on approach → Task: align without escalation → Action: 1-on-1 + data-driven discussion → Result: consensus reachedEQ, communication, maturityStay objective, no blame
Handling Failure"Tell me about your biggest failure and what you learned."Situation: production bug → Task: fix + communicate → Action: owned mistake, RCA, process change → Result: no repeat incidentsSelf-awareness, growth mindsetReal failure, not humble-brag
Prioritization"How do you handle multiple high-priority tasks?"Situation: competing deadlines → Task: decide order → Action: impact/effort matrix + stakeholder alignment → Result: critical delivered firstTime mgmt, judgment, communicationUse frameworks: RICE, MoSCoW
Cross-team Collaboration"Describe a time you worked across teams to deliver a feature."Situation: feature needing 3 teams → Task: coordinate → Action: set up sync cadence, tracked dependencies → Result: launched togetherCollaboration, stakeholder mgmtHighlight your proactive role
Ambiguity"Tell me about a time you had to make a decision with incomplete info."Situation: unclear requirement → Task: decide direction → Action: gathered minimum inputs, made reversible decision → Result: validated fastRisk tolerance, pragmatismShow decision-making process
Innovation / Impact"What's the most impactful thing you've built or improved?"Situation: slow deploy pipeline → Task: improve CI/CD → Action: automated, reduced to 10 min → Result: 40% faster releasesInitiative, technical judgmentQuantify the impact always
Mentorship / Growth"Tell me about a time you mentored someone."Situation: junior struggling → Task: support without doing for them → Action: weekly 1-on-1s, pair programming → Result: promoted in 6 monthsEmpathy, coaching, investmentShow long-term thinking
Customer / User Focus"Describe a time you advocated for the user."Situation: feature misaligned with users → Task: push back on spec → Action: user research, data presented → Result: redesigned featureEmpathy, courage, data-drivenTie back to business outcome
Ownership"Tell me about a time you went beyond your role."Situation: gap in ownership → Task: fill it without being asked → Action: picked it up, fixed the issue → Result: saved project timelineInitiative, accountabilityKey for senior+ roles
Disagreement with Manager"Have you ever disagreed with your manager? What happened?"Situation: different tech choice → Task: express concern → Action: presented trade-offs, escalated respectfully → Result: compromise reachedConfidence, respect, diplomacyNever badmouth; show process
Career / Motivation"Why do you want to join us / leave your current company?"Situation: current growth ceiling → Task: next challenge → Action: researched company, aligned goals → Result: clear pull reasonsSelf-awareness, culture fitBe honest; push, not just pull
